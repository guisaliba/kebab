# Page Types

## overview
High-level map or navigation page.

## concept
Definition or durable explanation.

## tactic
Specific actionable technique.

## playbook
Step-by-step operational workflow.

## platform
Knowledge scoped to a specific ecosystem or tool.

## metric
Definition, formula, interpretation and caveats.

## framework
Named evaluation model or mental model.

## creator
Source-author page that aggregates recurring views and reliability notes.

## source-note
Summary page for one source, linking into the rest of the wiki.

## comparison
Contrast page between approaches, sources or frameworks.

## qa
Durable reusable answer to a recurring question.

## decision
Page documenting a durable editorial or governance decision.
