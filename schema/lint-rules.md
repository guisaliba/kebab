# Lint Rules

Check for:

- orphan wiki pages
- missing citations
- duplicate concept pages
- approved pages with low confidence and no caveat
- source notes with no links into the wiki
- unresolved contradictions older than threshold
- malformed citation markers that do not match `[Sources: ...]`
- any page with `confidence: low` and `review_status: approved` that lacks a non-empty `## Caveats` section
