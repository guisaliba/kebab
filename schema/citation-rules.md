# Citation Rules

- Every nontrivial claim in `wiki/` must cite at least one `source_id`.
- Citations should point to chunk, section, chapter, or timestamp when possible.
- Avoid vague attribution such as "the course says".
- When a claim is context-bound, note the caveat in prose rather than pretending universality.
- Standard inline citation marker for linting: `[Sources: SRC-2026-0001 §lesson-01 00:12:10-00:15:42; SRC-2026-0008 ch.03]`.
